FACT #1

During Stan lee 's childhood days, he used to read the storybooks such as Mark Twain, Jules Verne, The hardy boys, Tarzan of the apes. As he got older he used to read the stories and poems written by famous authors and writers like Edgar Allan Poe, Arthur Conan Doyle, Shakespeare and many more.

FACT #5

FACT #6

His favorite early job was that of an usher at the Rivoli theater in times square. He had a great experience working there. There he met a very famous person Eleanor Roosevelt. Meeting Eleanor Roosevelt was a memorable experience in his life.


FACT #7

He started his career in comic writing with the Mcgraw hill publishing company. He started the job of an assistant to Jack Kirby and Joe Simon. But soon his life changed and he became in charge of the comics section.


FACT #8
